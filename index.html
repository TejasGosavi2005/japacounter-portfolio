A devotional Android app that helps you stay consistent with your mantra chanting – with custom mantras, voice-based japa counting, streaks and gentle reminders.

Who Can Use JapaCounter Pro?

Daily Sadhana Practitioners

Track your daily naam japa without losing focus, just like using a digital japmala.

Students & Professionals

Busy schedule? Use reminders and streaks to keep your chanting regular even on hectic days.

Group / Family Japa

Use one device to keep a shared count during satsang, bhajans or family japa sessions.

New to Mantra Chanting

Start small, chant with intention, and slowly build your habit with simple, visual tracking.

Key Features of the App

Custom Mantra Input

You can type and save your own mantra in English or any language of your choice. The app remembers your mantra so you can continue your japa anytime.

Voice Japa Counting

Speak your mantra and let the app listen. Whenever your mantra is detected, the counter automatically increases – perfect for hands-free chanting.

Daily Reminder Notifications

Set gentle notifications so you never forget your japa. Morning, evening or any time that fits your personal routine.

Streak & Progress Tracking

JapaCounter Pro maintains your daily streak and total count, so you can see how consistent your sadhana has been over time.

How the App Works

1. Set Your Mantra

Open the app, type your preferred mantra (for example “Hare Krishna”, “Om Namah Shivaya”) and save it as your current mantra.

2. Start Japa – Tap or Voice

Use Tap to Count for simple digital mala experience or turn on Voice Japa mode so that chanting aloud automatically increases your count.

3. Track Your Progress

Check your total japa, daily streak and notifications. Over days and weeks, you can see how your discipline and devotion are growing.
